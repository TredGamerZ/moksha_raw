About Moksha

Moksha is the annual inter-college cultural festival of Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology. Started in 2003, it is organized in the month of March. It is a three-day cultural extravaganza held every even semester of the academic year. It is considered to be one of the biggest cultural fests in north India & attracts students from top colleges all over the country.

Moksha presents a platform for the students to break free from their monotonous life. The fest has an average footfall of over 25000 people and a lineup of around 50 engaging events.
