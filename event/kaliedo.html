Kaleidoscope

The Poster Making Competition

Painting is silent poetry. Poster making is to present your imagination in a symbolic & comprehensive manner. Canvas, the art & craft society of NSIT has brought you Kaleidoscope -the poster making competition. The event will take place on 15th March 2018, 01:00 pm onwards.

Guidelines & Rules:

  1. On spot registration is allowed.

  2. Theme will be provided on the spot.

  3. 2-hour time will be given to each participant.

  4. Sheets will be provided to the participants.

  5. Participants have to bring their colours & brushes.

  6. Single participant is allowed against each entry.

  7. Mobile phones and other references are not allowed during event.

  8. Participants need to reach half an hour before 01:00 PM at Student Activity Center (SAC) in case of on spot registration.
