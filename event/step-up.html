Step UP

THE STREET DANCE COMPETITION

The street dance competition of NSIT is back once again to get your blood racing, hearts pumping and tempers soaring.
Can your crew go toe-to-toe and battle your way to the top? So, If the Street is your stage, then its time to STEP UP!

Rules for the event

  1. Team size of 5-25 people. Students who are registered in any program of an educational Institute of India and have a valid identity card can participate in the event.

  2. Participants must incorporate any style(s) from Hip Hop, Popping, Tutting, Crumping, Break dance, Stepping, Contraptions and Western Freestyle in their dance performance.

  3. The teams must bring their track in a pen-drive in audio format (.mp3).

  4. In all cases, the judges' decision will be final and binding.

Rounds

  1. Eliminations Round
    Teams need to perform a dance sequence without any theme for the same.
    Time limit: 4-7 min (+1 min setup time).

  2. Semi final and Final round: (Street Dance Battle Ground)
    The shortlisted teams will then have a face-off in the battle round.
    Time limit: 6-8 minutes(+1 min setup time).
