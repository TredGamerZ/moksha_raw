The Short Film Competition

The Short Film Competition being held in the Annual Cultural Fest of NSIT - Moksha'18, is being organized by Teen Pathar Productions - a private limited production house, in association with TheatreCon - the Theatre Fest of NSIT. By entering and participating in the contest, you agree to be bound by the Rules and Regulations.
There will be a screening of the Top 10 selected teams in college during the fest Moksha on 28th March 2018. All filmmakers/teams must be present for the screening.

Eligibility

  1. The filmmaker/team participating should be college students.

  2. The age restriction is 18 years and above for the participants.

  3. The participant shall participate in the contest by submitting an entry form, along with their short film by 23rd March 2018

  4. Any kind of content used in the short film that is not original like music, background scores, lyrics, sound records, etc. should be given due credit at the end. 

  5. Each participant shall be eligible to only send one entry.

The Short Film

  1. The short film must be submitted online, through the entry form - in any of the links, Google Drive, YouTube or Vimeo.

  2. The short film should not exceed 15 minutes in duration, including titles and credits.

  3. The file can be in any format, preferably MP4.

  4. The short film may be made in any Indian language. If it is not in Hindi or English, subtitles are a must.

Award Categories

  1. Best Film (1st Prize)

  2. Second Best Film (2nd Prize)
