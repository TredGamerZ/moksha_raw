Rules for Preliminary Qualifications

  1. There will only be 1 round .

  2. Each team will get 12 minutes for performing and will be disqualified after 15 minutes. From 12th to 15th minute points will be deducted accordingly.

  3. The participants (teams) are required to bring their own track in USB drives only.

  4. The teams should carry multiple copies of their sound tracks in multiple pen drives(2-3) to avoid any discrepancy during the event.

  5. All the participants should carry their college ID cards.

  6. The registered teams should be physically present on the day of event and no online entries of the performances would be entertained.

  7. Do not rely upon us for providing you with the additional props needed in your performance.(some basic props like chairs, escorts for handling your props would be provided).

  8. The teams should report atleast 2 hours prior to the event.
