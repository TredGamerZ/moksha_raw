Symphony

The Instrumental Duo/Trio Competition

Music, no matter how beautifully sung, is incomplete without the musical instruments they’re sung to. Dhwani’18 @ Moksha brings to you Symphony - The Instrumental Duo/Trio Competition, to be held on 28th March, 2018 at 3pm.

Guidelines:

  1. Only 2 entries per college are allowed.

  2. Time limit is 5+2 minutes.

  3. Participants are to bring their own instruments.

  4. Use of drum kit is prohibited.

  5. Maximum team size is 3.

  6. Pre-recorded backing tracks are not allowed. Live looping is allowed.

  7. There is no limitation on the genre of music to be presented.

  8. Judgement will be based on the qualities like dynamics, expression, phrasing, rhythm, technique, tone quality, etc.

  9. Judge’s decision will be final and binding.

  10. No preliminary round will be held for the event.

Rules for Preliminary Qualifications

  1. Those interested are required to fill this form

  2. Entries of a minimum of 3 minutes and maximum of 7 minutes time should be sent to dhwani.moksha@gmail.com .

  3. Entries are to be sent latest by 20th March, 2018.

  4. Entries can be of audio/video formats. Teams may send a link to a youtube video or a soundcloud audio of their previous performance.

  5. Drums are not allowed in the preliminary as well as the final rounds.

  6. There is no limitation on the genre of music to be presented.

  7. Judgement shall not be based on quality of audio/video.

  8. Mention the subject of the email as ‘SYMPHONY’18 REGISTRATION’.

  9. Participants are requested to mention their names, college name, email ID, contact numbers and list of instruments used in the video/audio in the preliminary registration email as well.
