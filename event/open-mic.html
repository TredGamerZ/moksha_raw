The Open Mic Event

Hola! Instrumentalists, writers, singers, storytellers, poets, jugglers and magicians and budding comedians!
It’s time to put away all stress in your life and be a part of this most happening event of Moksha. Immerse yourself in an evening of slam poetry, stand-up comedy, and live music. There are no constraints, no language barriers. The stage is yours. The mic is open to all. Now flaunt us your skills!

Rules for the event

  1. The content to be performed must be original. Any form of plagiarism will lead to disqualification.

  2. Participants will be given minimum 1 and maximum 4 minutes to perform.

  3. Due to limited time, performances will be based on first come first served basis.

  4. Each performance will be videographed and the best ones will be published with due credits to the participants.
