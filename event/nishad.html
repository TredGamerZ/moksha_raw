NISHAD

The Unplugged Night

IT IS TIME! Time to stand up and take over the stage with your music, beat box and poetry.
We have you covered with a cheerful audience to encourage you. Show us your songwriting skills and your vibe to light up people's heart. We invite to come chill and listen to the evening acoustics with your friends and loved ones on the Day-1 of Delhi's biggest college cultural event - "MOKSHA '18"! This is an open mic event where anyone can showcase their love for music and poetry in a relaxed environment.

Rules for the event

  1. We will be providing you with :-
    3 Microphones
    1 Acoutic Guitar
    1 Cajon
    1 Keyboard

  2. We ask that you limit your acts to 10 minutes or less.

  3. Any additional equipments will not be provided and have to be arranged personally. Free soft drinks and juices will be available too.

  4. There is no prior registration for this event.
