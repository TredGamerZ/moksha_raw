QUERENCIA

Chess Tournament

Shatranj, the Chess Club of NSIT in association with Venatus presents QUERENCIA. A one of a kind blitz chess tournament that gives everybody a chance to show their intellectual prowess. Join us on March 28 to participate.

Rules for the event

  1. Time controls for each player in a game of blitz chess are, according to FIDE, 5 minutes per player.

  2. Can be "Sudden death", with no time increment per move.

  3. The play will be governed by the FIDE Laws of Chess.

  4. If a player makes an illegal move, the player's opponent may point it out and claim a win.

  5. In case of a dispute during a tournament, either player may stop the clock and call the arbiter to make a final and binding judgment.
