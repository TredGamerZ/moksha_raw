In our attempt to bring about change in the society through street plays, ASHWAMEDH-The Dramatics Society of NSIT brings to you Soch - The Street Play event, under Theatrecon : The Dramatics Festival of NSIT as a part of its annual cultural fest, Moksha ‘18 at NSIT Delhi.

Guidelines:

  1. Fill up the given form on the right side.

  2. You are all set for now. A confirmation mail will be sent to you on the email id provided.

  3. Last Date To Register : 8th March, 2018, 11:59 pm.

  4. No entries will be accepted after this.

  5. Once you've registered with our event, you shall be notified regarding the next step of registration on your official e-mail ID.

Rules and Regulations:

  1. Maximum 23+2 members per team (including instrumentalists).

  2. Maximum time limit: 20+2 minutes (Empty stage to empty stage).

  3. Negative marking for exceeding the time limit.

  4. Use of foul and derogatory language is not acceptable.

  5. Usage of fire, water or any other potentially harmful substance is strictly prohibited.

  6. The teams should have their own properties and instruments.

  7. Only live music is allowed.

  8. The performance should be in Hindi (short snippets in other languages are allowed).

  9. Decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
