Fake Off

Does Atlas hold up the world? Is 1 _really_ greater than 0? Distort the truth and fiddle around with various mathematical and astronomical theories, and try your best to contrive coherent details to prove untrue scenarios.
Nakshatra - The Astronomy and Mathematics Society of NSIT presents 'Fake Off', where the goal is to make the audience believe the unbelievable. Each team must use their logical, analytical and persuasive skills, to prove correct an idea, which is based on incorrect grounds.

Rules for the event

  1. Participate in teams of 1-2, wherein cross college teams are allowed

  2. There would be a strict presentation time of 5 minutes.

  3. Decision of judges would be final and should be abided by all the participants.

  4. On the spot topics would be provided to the team.

  5. No Prior registration is required for the event.
